About Swastik Logistics

Swastik Logistics is a premier provider of specialized transportation services for bulk bitumen and emulsion across India. With over a decade of industry experience, we ensure safe, reliable, and efficient delivery solutions for our clients.

Established in 2010, Swastik Logistics has grown to become one of the most trusted names in bitumen transportation. We've expanded our fleet from 5 to over 50 specialized tankers, serving clients across 15 states in India.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and safe bitumen transportation solutions while building lasting partnerships with our clients through exceptional service.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted logistics partner for petroleum and construction materials, setting industry benchmarks for safety and reliability.
