About Swastik Logistics

Swastik Logistics is a premier provider of specialized transportation services for bulk bitumen and emulsion across India. With years of industry experience, we ensure safe, reliable, and efficient delivery solutions for our clients.

As authorized transport partners for leading petroleum companies including BPCL, IOCL, HPCL, and government departments like PWD Maharashtra, we maintain the highest standards of service quality and safety compliance.

Our modern fleet of temperature-controlled tankers and experienced logistics team enables us to handle complex transportation requirements with precision and professionalism.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and safe bitumen transportation solutions while building lasting partnerships with our clients through exceptional service.
